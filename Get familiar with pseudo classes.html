Choreography Meaning

1.Bramhanjali:
Brahmanjali is the traditional opening invocatory piece in a Kuchipudi recital, serving as a
prayer to seek blessings from the Divine, gurus, and the audience before performing.
Choreographed by Dr. Vempati Chinna Satyam, it is the first dance item a student learns,
combinig devotion with pure movement...

2.Koluvaithiva Ranga Sai:
"Koluvaithiva Ranga Sai" is a popular Kuchipudi dance item, choreographed by Dr.
Vempati Chinna Satyam, that translates to "Oh Lord Ranganatha (Vishnu), are you
seated in royal splendor?". It is a devotional piece portraying the divine beauty and
grandeur of Lord Vishnu reclining on Adisesha, often expressing that a thousand eyes
are not enough to behold his glory...

3.Brindavana Nilaye:
"Brindavana Nilaye" is a popular Kuchipudi dance piece (kriti) composed by Oothukadu
Venkata Subba Iyer, meaning "She who resides in Brindavan" (Brindavana =
Brindavan; Nilaye = Resider/Abode). It is a devotional piece addressed to Radha,
describing her deep love for Lord Krishna in the sacred city of Brindavan...
