Section 1

Codingal

Coding, also known as computer programming, is the process of
writing step-by-step instructions that tell a computer or machine
exactly how to perform a task. Just like a chef uses a recipe to
bake a cake, a programmer uses code to build software, apps, and
websites.

Section 2

More about Coding

Advanced coding is the composition of optimized, scalable software architecture. It
focuses on designing efficient algorithms, managing complex data structures, and
implementing design patterns. Rather than just writing functional commands, it
involves building robust, high-performance, and maintainable systems using modern
frameworks and APIs
